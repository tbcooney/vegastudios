WHO WE ARE

We are Vega Studios

Great design is the purpose. Vega Studios creates cultural impact on behalf of brands. Clients are provided with effective, efficient as well as attractive branding designs that give them a competitive edge. We pride ourselves not only on the work we put out, but also on our service capabilities. We bring a unique perspective that focuses on fresh ideas and interactive experiences. No matter your budget, we ensure all of your key marketing & branding initiatives are fulfilled.
We combine technical expertise and business sense to develop impactful creative platforms. The advantage is through our network of like-minded creatives that can service all company needs. Going well beyond advice and opinion, clients benefit from collaborative actions that provide contemporary insight and execution. You will have access to a small team of designers, UX strategists and developers that will work to fulfill your companies needs.

WHAT WE DO

OUR SERVICES

We're a fully distributed team that works from all over Canada. We use an iterative process and can fit in directly with your team to deliver designs that will help your company grow. Our service offerings encompass the following disciplines:

Web Design

We envision, design and engineer responsive websites that allow customers to connect on a functional and emotional level.

Branding & Identity

We translate Brand Strategy into verbal and visual expressions, from corporate identity to digital experiences.

Print Design

To create signature advertising experiences, we envsion and design across multiple print mediums including magazines, newspapers, billboards and posters.

Presentation Design

We can help in developing dynamic content that turns your business presentations into something memorable.

User Interface Design

Our clean and sleek designs provide a coherent framework for creating brand expressions and product experiences.

Information Architecture

Our visual designers and UX specialists work together to create elegant, useful, and unique solutions for you to build around.
